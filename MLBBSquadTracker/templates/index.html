MLBB Squad Manager Bot

Bot Status: Online

About

This is a Discord bot for managing MLBB (Mobile Legends: Bang Bang) squads and players.

Features

  • Manage squads and their members
  • Register players with their MLBB IDs
  • Track free agents (players without squads)
  • Search for players and squads
  • View detailed player profiles

Commands

  • !squad_create <name> <description> - Create a new squad
  • !squad_update <name> <description> - Update squad details
  • !squad_delete <name> - Delete a squad
  • !add_member <squad_name> <@user> <mlbb_id> <mlbb_username> <role> - Add member to squad
  • !remove_member <squad_name> <@user> - Remove member from squad
  • !update_member <@user> <field> <value> - Update member information

  • !register <mlbb_id> <mlbb_username> - Register as a player
  • !profile - Show your profile
  • !profile_update <field> <value> - Update your profile

  • !squads - List all squads
  • !squad_info <name> - Show squad details
  • !free_agents - List all players without squads
  • !search_player <name or id> - Search for a player
